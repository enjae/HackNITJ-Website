About HackNITJ

HACKNITJ is being organized to shortlist the teams for Smart India Hackathon 2022, (SIH'22). The shortlisted teams will represent NIT Jalandhar at the National Level. The shortlisted teams will get to represent NIT Jalandhar at National Level.The teams will compete against each other and time to showcase the best solutions by daunting the programming skills and win huge prizes.

FAQS

  • Is there a particular theme for the Hackathon?
    • The themes are listed above in Themes section. You can work on any theme from the ones listed there.
  • Are there any registration charges?
    • No. Registrations are completely free of cost.
  • What is the criteria of selection?
    • You will be judged on the quality of your idea. The idea is only a part of the judgement. The skills of your team members would also play a major role in your selection.
  • Is the selection per-member or per-team basis?
    • The selection is per-team basis.
  • How do we have to submit our idea?
    • You must submit your idea as a powerpoint presentation with the format provided. You can also download the PPT format by clicking here.
  • I don't have an original idea. Can I still participate?
    • Yes. Not every idea needs to be unique. Sometimes you may do it better than existent. You can also improve the existing products.
  • I don't have a Computer Science background. Can I still participate?
    • Yes. Creativity is independent of your technical skills. You can learn the tools and technologies anytime.
  • Where can I find complete details of the event?
    • You are requested to go through the Rule Book and the Code of Conduct to get the complete details of the event.
  • Do we get any certificate for participation?
    • Yes, you do get a certificate of participation if you qualify for the finals after the initial shortlisting.
  • I don't find my question listed here. What can I do?
    • You can always contact us. We would be more than happy to help you.
